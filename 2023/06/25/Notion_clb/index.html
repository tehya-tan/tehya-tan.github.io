Leveraging Collaboration in Notion

Posted on 2023-06-25 Edited on 2023-07-01 In Tech Views: Disqus:

Example of organizing exam solutions

1 Benefits of Collaboration

  1. Workload Reduction:
    • Collaborating with others can alleviate the burden of creating comprehensive notes.
    • By inviting individuals to edit the same page, we can distribute the workload and ensure a more efficient note-taking process.
  2. Error Correction:
    • Making mistakes while taking notes is common.
    • By inviting others to co-edit the notes, we enhance chances of identifying and rectifying these errors.
    • Leveraging the "comment" function on the Notion page allows for effective error detection and correction.

2 Example of Notion Collaboration

Here I share an example when I prepared for the qualifying exam.

Since there is no official solution for the past exam problems, I think it would be interesting and advantageous to gather solutions from various sources, and organizing them into a reliable and comprehensive collection.

This collection, which appears as a Notion page, will

  1. benefit my colleagues and me in our exam preparation
  2. provide valuable resources for future students undertaking the same examination.

Recognizing the extensive effort required to build this collection, I have invited my colleagues to contribute in the following ways:

  1. Uploading solutions for unsolved problems to distribute the workload.
  2. Potentially correcting solutions provided by others using the comment feature.

In the notion page I created, all the problems from the qualifying exams in the past 4 years are organized by date and subject. (template is here, details are omitted in the template)

Problem and Solution: each problem has a toggle list, which includes the original problem and the solutions from different people.

Comment: Access to the comment feature is available to everyone on this page. If you have concerns or difficulties understanding a solution, simply mark the relevant section and ask your question through comments. The commenting feature supports inline math and picture uploads, facilitating convenient clarification and interactive discussions.

Subject Glance: For a specific subject, you can quickly look over all the related problems, providing a comprehensive view and facilitating analysis of the problem scope within that subject.

Publish: After the exam, I also utilize the publish feature on our collaborative Notion page to share it with other students who are preparing to take the same exam. By enabling the allow commentsoption, anyone with the link can access the page and provide valuable comments and insights.
